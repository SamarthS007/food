Top 10 Food Items Famous in India

1. Biryani

Biryani is a popular rice dish mixed with spices, meat, and vegetables.

2. Masala Dosa

Masala Dosa is a crispy pancake filled with a spicy potato mixture.

3. Butter Chicken

Butter Chicken is a creamy and flavorful chicken dish cooked in a rich tomato sauce.

4. Chaat

Chaat is a savory snack made with a mix of fried dough, tangy sauces, and spices.

5. Chole Bhature

Chole Bhature is a combination of spicy chickpea curry and fried bread.

6. Rogan Josh

Rogan Josh is a fragrant lamb curry cooked with a blend of spices.

7. Paneer Tikka

Paneer Tikka is a popular vegetarian dish made with marinated paneer and grilled.

8. Vada Pav

Vada Pav is a street food snack consisting of a spicy potato patty in a bun.

9. Pani Puri

Pani Puri is a popular street food snack made with crispy hollow puris filled with flavored water.

10. Jalebi

Jalebi is a sweet deep-fried dessert made by swirling batter in a pretzel shape and soaking it in sugar syrup.
